Welcome to Dice Empire

These are the rules:

- The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
- In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his ROUND score
- BUT, if the player rolls a 1 and double 6, all his ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn
- The player can choose to 'Hold', so his ROUND score gets added to his GLOBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn
- The first player to reach the inputed final score or the default 100 points on GLOBAL score (if no final score is given) wins the game.
